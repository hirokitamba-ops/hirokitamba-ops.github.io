Decision Without Deciders: AI Governance and the Infrastructuralization of Accountability

Author: Hiroki Tamba

DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18399999

Abstract

This preprint develops a structural theory of AI governance centered on the concept of "decision without deciders." Rather than treating AI accountability as a problem of regulatory design, policy implementation, or institutional responsibility, the paper analyzes how accountability itself is being infrastructuralized across contemporary socio-technical systems.
