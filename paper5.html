ORCID: 0009-0004-7635-0741

Abstract

This preprint develops a case-based calibration approach for the TLIM analytical framework. Rather than treating TLIM as a purely abstract model, this study demonstrates how empirical cases can be used to refine and calibrate its analytical components. By applying TLIM to multiple domains, the paper identifies recurring patterns and deviations that inform the adjustment of model parameters. This calibration process enhances the explanatory power of TLIM while maintaining its general applicability. The approach emphasizes iterative interaction between theory and case material, allowing the framework to evolve through empirical engagement.
