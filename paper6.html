Abstract

This preprint outlines the TLIM Research Program as an integrated approach to studying predictive classification, causation, and semantic governance. The program brings together theoretical development, empirical analysis, and methodological refinement within a unified framework. It positions predictive classification as a central mechanism through which contemporary systems shape behaviour and organize knowledge. By integrating multiple analytical dimensions, the program aims to establish a coherent research agenda that bridges disciplines and enables cumulative knowledge production.

Part of the TLIM Research Program. For foundational definition see: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18637708
